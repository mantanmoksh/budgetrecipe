Instructions

In a bowl, mix gram flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder (if using), asafoetida, and salt. Add a little water at a time to make a smooth, medium-thick batter.
Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Dip each spinach leaf into the batter, ensuring it's evenly coated.
Carefully drop the coated spinach leaves into the hot oil, a few at a time. Fry them until they are crisp and golden brown.
Remove the fried spinach leaves with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
To assemble the Palak Chaat: Arrange the crispy spinach leaves on a plate.
Drizzle whisked yogurt generously over the spinach leaves.
Spoon tamarind chutney and green chutney over the yogurt.
Sprinkle finely chopped onions and tomatoes over the chutneys.
Top with a generous amount of sev.
Finally, sprinkle chaat masala and garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors!
