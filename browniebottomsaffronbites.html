Instructions

Prep: Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving overhang for easy removal. Soak saffron in 1 tbsp warm milk and set aside.
Brownie Layer: Melt butter and whisk with sugar. Add eggs one at a time. Sift in flour and cocoa powder, mixing just until combined. Spread evenly in prepared pan.
Cheesecake Layer: Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar, then eggs one at a time. Mix in vanilla and saffron milk (with threads). Pour over brownie layer.
Bake: Bake for 35-40 minutes until edges are set but center still jiggles slightly. Cool completely in pan, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight).
Serve: Use parchment to lift from pan. Cut into 16 squares. Garnish with edible gold leaf or crushed pistachios if desired. Store refrigerated for up to 5 days.
