Instructions

In a bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, finely chopped onion, chopped green chili (if using), turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
Spread the potato mixture evenly on one slice of bread.
Place the other slice of bread on top, making a sandwich.
Heat butter or oil in a pan or on a griddle over medium heat.
Place the potato sandwich on the hot pan and cook on both sides until golden brown and crispy. Press down gently with a spatula to ensure even cooking.
Once the aloo toast is cooked to your liking, remove it from the pan and cut it into halves or triangles.
Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired, and serve hot with your favorite chutney or sauce.
