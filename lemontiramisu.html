Instructions

In a medium bowl, whisk together the mascarpone cheese, heavy cream, and granulated sugar until smooth and slightly thickened.
Stir in the lemon juice and lemon zest until well combined. If using limoncello, lightly brush the ladyfingers with it.
Arrange a layer of limoncello-brushed ladyfingers (if using) or plain ladyfingers on the bottom of a serving dish or individual glasses.
Spread half of the mascarpone-lemon mixture evenly over the ladyfingers.
Arrange another layer of ladyfingers over the cream mixture.
Spread the remaining mascarpone-lemon mixture over the second layer of ladyfingers.
Cover the dish and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld and the ladyfingers to soften.
Before serving, garnish with additional lemon zest or a sprinkle of powdered sugar, if desired.
