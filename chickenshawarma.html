Instructions

In a bowl, combine the thinly sliced chicken with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, cayenne pepper, and salt.
Mix well to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the marinade.
Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to a few hours for a more intense flavor.
Heat a large skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
Add the marinated chicken to the hot pan in a single layer. Avoid overcrowding the pan; cook in batches if necessary.
Cook the chicken for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until it's cooked through and lightly browned.
Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pan and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it thinly.
Warm the pita bread lightly on a pan or in the microwave.
Spread tahini sauce or yogurt sauce inside the pita bread.
Fill the pita bread with the sliced chicken shawarma, shredded lettuce, chopped tomatoes, and sliced onions.
Wrap it up and serve immediately. You can also add other toppings like pickles or hummus.
