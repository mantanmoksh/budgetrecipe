Instructions

Step 1: Cut or crumble pre-made brownies into bite-sized pieces. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare chocolate pudding according to the package instructions (or use ready-made pudding). Allow it to cool if freshly made.

Step 3: In a trifle dish or individual serving glasses, create a layer of brownie pieces at the bottom.

Step 4: Add a layer of chocolate pudding over the brownies, spreading it evenly.

Step 5: Spread a layer of whipped cream on top of the pudding.

Step 6: Repeat the layers (brownie, pudding, whipped cream) until the dish is filled, finishing with whipped cream on the top layer.

Step 7: Garnish with chocolate shavings, fresh berries, or a sprinkle of cocoa powder for a decorative touch.

Step 8: Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours to allow the flavors to meld together. Serve chilled and enjoy!
