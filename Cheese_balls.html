Instructions

In a mixing bowl, combine the grated cheddar cheese and mashed potato. Mix well until they are evenly combined.
Add cornflour, all-purpose flour, mixed herbs, black pepper powder, and salt to the cheese and potato mixture. Mix everything together to form a soft dough.
Take a small portion of the dough and roll it between your palms to form a smooth ball (about 1-inch in diameter). Repeat with the remaining dough.
Place the breadcrumbs on a plate. Roll each cheese ball in the breadcrumbs until they are evenly coated.
**For Frying:** Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai over medium heat. Carefully drop the cheese balls into the hot oil in batches, ensuring not to overcrowd the pan.
Fry the cheese balls for 2-3 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy.
Remove the fried cheese balls with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. Serve hot.
**For Baking (Optional):** Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Place the coated cheese balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. Serve warm.
