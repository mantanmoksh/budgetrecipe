Instructions

In a mixing bowl, whisk together the gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder (if using), cumin powder, and salt.
Gradually add water, whisking continuously to form a smooth, lump-free batter. The batter should have a medium-thin consistency.
If using, add the chopped onion, green chili, and coriander leaves to the batter and mix well.
Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat. Grease it lightly with oil.
Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin pancake (chilla).
Cook on medium heat for 1-2 minutes until the bottom turns light golden brown. Drizzle a little oil around the edges.
Flip the chilla carefully and cook the other side for another 1-2 minutes until it's cooked through and lightly browned.
Remove the besan chilla from the pan. Repeat with the remaining batter.
Serve hot with chutney, sauce, or yogurt.
