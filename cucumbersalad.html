Instructions

Slice the Cucumbers: Wash the cucumbers thoroughly. You can peel them if you prefer, or leave the skin on for added texture and nutrients. Thinly slice the cucumbers.
Prepare the Onion: Thinly slice the red onion. If you find the raw onion flavor too strong, you can soak the slices in cold water for a few minutes, then drain them.
Combine Vegetables: In a medium bowl, combine the sliced cucumbers and red onion.
Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the white vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper until the sugar and salt are dissolved.
Dress the Salad: Pour the dressing over the cucumber and onion mixture.
Toss and Chill: Gently toss the salad to ensure the cucumbers and onions are evenly coated with the dressing. For the best flavor, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 15-20 minutes before serving. This allows the flavors to meld together.
Serve: Once chilled, give the salad another gentle toss and serve as a refreshing side dish.
