Instructions

Cold Brew: Combine coffee grounds and cold water in a jar. Stir to ensure all grounds are wet. Cover and refrigerate for 12-24 hours (longer = stronger).
Strain: Line a fine-mesh strainer with cheesecloth or a coffee filter. Strain coffee into a clean container, pressing gently on grounds to extract all liquid.
Store: Transfer concentrate to an airtight container. Will keep refrigerated for up to 2 weeks.
Serve: Fill glass with ice. For each serving, mix equal parts coffee concentrate and water (adjust to taste). Add milk and sweetener if desired.
Quick Alternative: For immediate iced coffee, brew double-strength hot coffee and pour over ice. Add cold water if too strong.
