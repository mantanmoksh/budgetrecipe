Instructions

In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, lemon juice, yogurt, garam masala, and salt.
Mix well to coat the chicken evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours for better flavor.
Heat butter in a pan or pot over medium heat. Add finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
Stir in the tomato puree, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes are cooked through and the oil starts to separate.
Add the marinated chicken pieces to the gravy. Cook on medium heat until the chicken is cooked through, about 10-15 minutes. You can add a little water if the gravy becomes too thick.
Stir in the garam masala and crushed kasuri methi. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
Add fresh cream and mix well. Simmer for 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Adjust the gravy consistency by adding water if needed.
Check for salt and add more if required.
Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Serve hot with naan, roti, rice, or paratha.
