Instructions (For Filling)

Heat a little oil in a wok or small pan over medium-high heat.
Add grated ginger and minced garlic. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
Add a portion of shredded cabbage, shredded carrots, and thinly sliced capsicum. Stir-fry on high heat for 1-2 minutes until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp.
Add thinly sliced spring onions, soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and salt to taste. Stir-fry for another minute.
Remove the vegetable filling from the heat and let it cool completely.

Instructions (For Rolling)

Prepare a slurry by mixing a bit of all-purpose flour with a little water until it forms a thick paste. This will be used to seal the spring roll.
Place a spring roll wrapper on a clean, flat surface with one corner pointing towards you (diamond shape).
Place about 2-3 tablespoons of the cooled vegetable filling in the center of the lower half of the wrapper.
Fold the bottom corner over the filling, then fold in the left and right corners tightly.
Roll the spring roll up tightly towards the remaining top corner.
Apply a little of the flour slurry to the top corner to seal the spring roll completely.

Instructions (For Frying)

Heat oil for deep frying in a small kadhai or deep pan over medium-high heat. The oil should be hot enough for the spring roll to turn golden brown and crispy.
Gently slide the prepared spring roll into the hot oil.
Fry the spring roll for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until it is golden brown and crispy.
Remove the fried spring roll from the oil using a slotted spoon and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
Serve the hot veg spring roll with your favorite dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or tomato ketchup.
