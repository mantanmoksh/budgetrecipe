Instructions

In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, hot sauce, smoked paprika, garlic powder, cayenne pepper (if using), dried oregano, and salt.
Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss them thoroughly to ensure they are evenly coated with the marinade.
Let the chicken wings marinate for at least 20-30 minutes at room temperature, or for a longer period (up to a few hours) in the refrigerator for more intense flavor.
Preheat your grill to medium heat. Lightly oil the grill grates to prevent sticking.
Place the marinated chicken wings on the preheated grill.
Grill the wings for about 20-25 minutes, turning them occasionally, until they are cooked through and the skin is crispy and slightly charred. Ensure the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
If you prefer baking, preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the marinated wings on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Bake for 30-40 minutes, flipping halfway through, until cooked through and crispy.
Once cooked, remove the spicy chicken wings from the grill or oven.
Serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauce, such as blue cheese or ranch dressing, and celery sticks.
