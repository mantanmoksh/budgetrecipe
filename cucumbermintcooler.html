Instructions

Prepare Cucumbers: Wash and roughly chop the cucumbers. If using non-organic cucumbers, peel them first. Reserve a few thin slices for garnish.
Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine chopped cucumbers, mint leaves, water, honey/sugar (if using), lemon juice, and salt. Blend until completely smooth.
Strain: Pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth into a pitcher to remove pulp. Press with a spoon to extract all the liquid.
Chill: Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow flavors to meld. For quicker chilling, add ice cubes directly to the pitcher.
Serve: Pour into glasses filled with ice cubes. Garnish with cucumber slices and mint sprigs. Serve immediately.
