Instructions

Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
On one slice, arrange the thinly sliced cucumber, tomato, and onion.
Add lettuce leaves and green bell pepper slices (if using).
Place cheese slices on top of the vegetables (if using).
Sprinkle salt, black pepper powder, and chaat masala (if using) over the vegetables and cheese.
Cover with the other bread slice, buttered/mayo side down.
Gently press the sandwich. You can cut it into halves or triangles before serving.
Serve immediately and enjoy your fresh vegetable sandwich!
