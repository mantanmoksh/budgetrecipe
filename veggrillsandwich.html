Instructions

Spread green chutney evenly on one side of one bread slice.
On the chutney-spread bread slice, arrange the sliced cucumber, tomatoes, and onions.
Sprinkle a little chaat masala, salt, and black pepper over the vegetables (if using).
If using, place a cheese slice over the vegetables.
Cover with the other bread slice.
Apply butter or oil on the outer sides of both bread slices.
Heat a grill pan or sandwich maker over medium heat.
Place the sandwich on the hot grill pan or in the sandwich maker.
Grill for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread turns golden brown and crispy, and the filling is slightly warm. If using a sandwich maker, follow the manufacturer's instructions.
Once grilled, remove the sandwich from the pan or sandwich maker.
Slice the sandwich in half diagonally or straight.
Serve the veg grill sandwich immediately with ketchup or more green chutney on the side.
