Instructions

Make the crust: In a food processor, pulse flour, powdered sugar, and salt. Add cold butter and pulse until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add egg yolk and pulse until dough comes together. Press into a 9-inch tart pan with removable bottom. Freeze for 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Bake chilled crust for 15-18 minutes until golden. Let cool completely.
Make the filling: In a saucepan, whisk together milk, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat until thickened. In a bowl, whisk egg, then gradually whisk in about 1/2 cup of the hot milk mixture. Return this to the saucepan and cook for 2 more minutes until very thick. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla. Cover with plastic wrap directly on surface and let cool.
Assemble: Spread cooled filling into cooled crust. Arrange fruit decoratively on top. Heat apricot jam with 1 teaspoon water and brush over fruit for glaze. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
Serving: Remove tart from pan by pushing up the bottom. Slice with a sharp knife dipped in hot water. Store leftovers covered in refrigerator for up to 2 days.
