Instructions

Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on your pizza dough package (usually around 220°C or 425°F).
Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
Arrange the sliced chicken, onion, and bell pepper over the cheese.
Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
Drizzle a little olive oil over the pizza.
Sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Instructions

Prepare the dough: Mix all-purpose flour, semolina, oil/ghee, and salt. Add water gradually to form a firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
Prepare the filling: Grind soaked lentils with spices. You can also add other fillings like peas or potatoes.
Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a small disc and place a spoonful of the filling in the center.
Bring the edges of the disc together to seal the filling inside, forming a ball again. Gently flatten it slightly.
Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
Gently slide the kachoris into the hot oil, a few at a time.
Fry them on medium-low heat until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides. This should take about 5-7 minutes per batch.
Remove the fried kachoris with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
Serve hot with chutney, aloo sabzi, or raita.
