Instructions

Take puffed rice in a large bowl.
Add finely chopped onions, tomatoes, and boiled potatoes.
Pour tamarind chutney and green chutney over the mixture. Adjust the amount according to your taste.
If using, add finely chopped green chilies and red chili powder.
Sprinkle chaat masala and black salt over the mixture.
Add most of the sev, reserving some for garnishing.
Gently toss everything together until all the ingredients are well combined. Be careful not to make the bhel soggy.
Squeeze a bit of lemon juice over the bhel puri.
Garnish with the remaining sev and finely chopped coriander leaves.
Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious and crunchy bhel puri!
