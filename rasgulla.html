Instructions

Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. When it comes to a boil, add lemon juice gradually while stirring until milk curdles completely.
Strain the chenna (paneer) through a muslin cloth, rinse with cold water, and hang for 30 minutes to drain excess water.
Knead the chenna for 10-15 minutes until smooth. Divide into small equal portions and roll into smooth balls without cracks.
In a wide pan, make sugar syrup with sugar and water. When boiling, gently add the chenna balls and cover. Cook for 15-20 minutes.
Turn off heat, add rose water and cardamom powder. Let it cool completely before serving chilled.
