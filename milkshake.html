Instructions

Chill Ingredients: For the thickest shake, make sure your ice cream is slightly softened but still very cold, and your milk is chilled.
Blend Base: In a blender, combine ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract. Blend on low speed for 15-20 seconds until just combined.
Adjust Consistency: For thicker shake, add more ice cream 1/4 cup at a time. For thinner shake, add more milk 1 tbsp at a time.
Add Flavorings: If making flavored variations (chocolate, strawberry, etc.), add those ingredients now and pulse briefly to combine.
Serve Immediately: Pour into chilled glasses, top with whipped cream and your favorite toppings. Serve with a straw and spoon!
