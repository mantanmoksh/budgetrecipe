Instructions

Heat oil in a pan or skillet over medium heat. Add thinly sliced onion and bell pepper. Sauté until they are slightly softened, about 5 minutes.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
Add the crumbled or cubed paneer to the pan.
Sprinkle in turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt to taste. Mix gently and cook for 2-3 minutes until the paneer is lightly coated with the spices.
Warm the tortillas or wraps on a griddle or in a microwave until they are soft and pliable.
Place a portion of the spiced paneer and vegetable mixture in the center of each warm tortilla.
Fold in the sides of the tortilla and then roll it up tightly to form a wrap.
You can optionally lightly grill the wraps on a pan for a few seconds on each side for a crispy exterior.
Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired, and serve the paneer wraps immediately. You can also serve them with your favorite dips or sauces.
