Instructions

In a bowl, combine the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt. Mix well with your hands until all the ingredients are evenly incorporated.
Divide the chicken mixture into 1 equal portion and shape it into a patty, slightly larger than the burger bun.
Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
Place the chicken patty in the hot pan and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until it's cooked through and golden brown. Ensure the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
If using cheese, place a slice on top of the patty during the last minute of cooking and let it melt.
Lightly toast the burger bun slices on the same pan or in a toaster.
Spread your favorite sauce on both halves of the toasted bun.
Place the cooked chicken patty (with cheese, if used) on the bottom bun.
Top with lettuce leaves, tomato slices, and onion slices.
Place the top bun over the toppings and serve immediately.
