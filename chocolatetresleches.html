Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the chocolate cake mix according to the package instructions and bake it. Let the cake cool completely.

Step 2: Use a fork or skewer to poke holes all over the cake.

Step 3: In a bowl, mix together full-fat milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk. Pour the milk mixture slowly over the cake, ensuring it soaks into the holes.

Step 4: Spread whipped cream evenly over the top of the cake.

Step 5: Refrigerate the cake for at least 4 hours or overnight to let the flavors blend beautifully.

Step 6: Garnish with chocolate shavings, cocoa powder, or berries for a decorative touch. Serve chilled and enjoy!
