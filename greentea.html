Instructions

Heat Water: Bring water to about 160-180¬∞F (70-80¬∞C). Green tea should never be brewed with boiling water as it can make the tea bitter. If you don't have a thermometer, boil water and let it cool for 2 minutes.
Prepare Tea: Place green tea leaves in a teapot or tea infuser. If using a tea bag, place it in your cup.
Steep: Pour the hot water over the tea leaves or tea bag. Let steep for 2-3 minutes (less for delicate teas, more for stronger flavor). Don't oversteep as it can become bitter.
Remove Leaves: Remove the tea leaves or tea bag. If using loose leaves, strain the tea into your cup.
Serve: Add honey or lemon if desired. Enjoy immediately for maximum flavor and health benefits. For iced green tea, let it cool then pour over ice.
