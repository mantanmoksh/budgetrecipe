Instructions

Grease a square tray with ghee and keep aside. Mix milk powder and cocoa powder in a bowl.
Heat sugar and water in a pan to make sugar syrup. Cook until it reaches one-string consistency.
Add ghee to the syrup, then gradually add the milk powder-cocoa mixture while stirring continuously.
Cook on low heat until the mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan. Add cardamom powder.
Pour into the greased tray, level the top, and let it cool. Cut into squares once set.
