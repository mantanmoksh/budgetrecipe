Instructions

Dry Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Make a well in the center.
Wet Ingredients: In another bowl, beat the egg, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla. Mix well.
Combine: Pour the wet ingredients into the well of dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined (a few lumps are okay). Don't overmix.
Cooking: Heat a griddle or pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on top and edges look set (about 2-3 minutes).
Flip & Finish: Flip and cook the other side until golden brown (about 1-2 minutes more). Serve warm with maple syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
