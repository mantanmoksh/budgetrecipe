Instructions

Pour the sweetened condensed milk into a medium-sized mixing bowl.
Add the desiccated coconut to the bowl.
Stir the condensed milk and coconut together thoroughly until well combined. The mixture should be thick and slightly sticky.
Take a small amount of the mixture (about 1-2 tablespoons) and roll it between your palms to form a ball.
You can optionally roll the balls in extra desiccated coconut for a nice coating.
Place the finished coconut balls on a plate or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
Refrigerate the coconut balls for at least 30 minutes to allow them to firm up.
Once chilled, the coconut balls are ready to serve and enjoy! Store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
