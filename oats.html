Why You'll Love Simple Cooked Oats

  • ❤️ Healthy & Filling: A great source of fiber and energy.
  • ⏱️ Super Quick: Ready in just a few minutes.
  • 💰 Budget-Friendly: Oats are very affordable.
  • 🎨 Versatile: Endless possibilities for toppings and flavors.
  • 👍 Easy to Digest: Gentle on the stomach.

Delicious Variations

  • Sweet Oats: Add honey, maple syrup, fruits, nuts, and seeds.
  • Savory Oats: Add salt, vegetables (like peas or spinach), and spices.
  • Milk Oats: Cook with milk instead of water for a creamier texture.
  • Overnight Oats: Prepare the oats in advance by soaking them in liquid overnight.

Instructions

Combine Ingredients: In a saucepan, combine the oats and water (or milk). Add a pinch of salt if desired.
Cook the Oats: Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
Simmer: Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 3-5 minutes for rolled oats, or 1-2 minutes for quick-cooking oats, stirring occasionally until the oats are cooked and have reached your desired consistency. Add more liquid if needed.
Serve: Pour the cooked oats into a bowl.
Add Toppings (Optional): Top with your favorite additions such as fresh or dried fruits, nuts, seeds, honey, maple syrup, cinnamon, or any other desired toppings.
