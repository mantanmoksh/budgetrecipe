Instructions

Brew Espresso: Prepare 1 shot of espresso (about 1 oz) using an espresso machine. If you don't have an espresso machine, brew 1/2 cup of extra strong coffee using 2 tablespoons of ground coffee per 1/2 cup water.
Heat Milk: Pour milk into a small saucepan and heat over medium heat until steaming (about 150¬∞F or 65¬∞C). Alternatively, microwave the milk in a microwave-safe container for 45-60 seconds until hot but not boiling.
Froth Milk: Use a milk frother to create foam. If you don't have a frother, vigorously whisk the milk or shake it in a tightly sealed jar until foamy. The ideal ratio is about 1/3 foam to 2/3 steamed milk.
Combine: Pour the espresso or strong coffee into a large mug. Slowly pour the steamed milk into the mug, holding back the foam with a spoon. Then spoon the foam on top.
Sweeten & Serve: Add sugar or vanilla extract if desired. For extra flair, dust the top with cocoa powder or cinnamon. Serve immediately while hot.
