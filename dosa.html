Instructions

Soaking: Wash rice, urad dal and fenugreek seeds separately. Soak rice for 4-6 hours and urad dal with fenugreek for 2 hours.
Grinding: Drain water and grind urad dal to smooth fluffy batter. Grind rice separately to smooth paste. Mix both batters together with salt.
Fermentation: Cover and let batter ferment in warm place for 8-12 hours or overnight. Batter will rise and become slightly sour.
Cooking: Heat griddle/tawa. Pour ladle of batter and spread in circular motion to make thin crepe. Drizzle oil/ghee. Cook until golden and crisp.
Serving: Fold dosa and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. For masala dosa, add potato filling before folding.
