Instructions

Prepare Ingredients: Finely chop the onion, coriander leaves, and green chilies (if using). Keep them ready.
Mix Batter and Toppings: In a bowl, gently mix the chopped onions, coriander leaves, and green chilies into the dosa batter. Add salt to taste and stir well.
Heat Griddle: Heat a flat griddle or non-stick pan over medium heat. Ensure it's hot before pouring the batter.
Pour Batter: Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the hot griddle. Gently spread it in a circular motion to form a thick pancake (thicker than a dosa).
Cook One Side: Drizzle some oil around the edges and on top of the uthapam. Cook on medium heat until the bottom turns light golden brown (about 2-3 minutes).
Flip and Cook Other Side: Gently flip the uthapam using a spatula. Drizzle some more oil around the edges. Cook the other side until it also turns golden brown and the onions are cooked through (about 2-3 minutes). Press gently with the spatula to ensure even cooking.
Serve Hot: Once both sides are cooked and golden brown, remove the onion uthapam from the griddle. Serve hot with sambar, coconut chutney, or tomato chutney.
