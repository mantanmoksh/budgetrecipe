Instructions

Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch.
Heat ghee or oil in a heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat.
Add cumin seeds to the hot ghee/oil. Let them splutter for a few seconds.
Add the rinsed rice to the pot and sauté for a minute, stirring gently.
Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt to taste. Bring the water to a boil.
Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot tightly with a lid, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until all the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked.
Remove the pot from the heat and let it rest, covered, for 5 minutes. This allows the steam to finish cooking the rice.
Gently fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
