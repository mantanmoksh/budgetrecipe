Instructions

 
 In a large bowl, mix together the gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, ajwain, baking soda, and salt.  
 
 Gradually add water to the dry ingredients, mixing well to form a smooth, thick batter. Ensure there are no lumps. The consistency should be such that it coats the vegetables.  
 
 Add the sliced onions, potatoes, spinach, and green chilies (if using) to the batter. Mix gently until the vegetables are well coated.  
 
 Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or kadai over medium heat. The oil should be hot enough for the pakoras to fry evenly.  
 
 Drop small spoonfuls of the vegetable and batter mixture into the hot oil. Do not overcrowd the pan.  
 
 Fry the pakoras on medium heat until they are golden brown and crispy. This should take about 3-4 minutes per batch.  
 
 Remove the fried pakoras with a slotted spoon and place them on a paper towel to drain excess oil.  
 
 Serve hot with your favorite chutney or sauce. Enjoy!
