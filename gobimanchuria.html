Instructions

Wash and cut the cauliflower into medium-sized florets. Blanch them in hot water for 2-3 minutes and drain well.
Make a batter with all-purpose flour, corn flour, salt, and water. The batter should be thick enough to coat the florets.
Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Dip each floret in the batter and deep fry until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.
In another pan, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add chopped onions, garlic, and saut√© until translucent.
Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, and other sauces. Mix well and add the fried cauliflower. Toss gently to coat.
Garnish with spring onions and serve hot as an appetizer or with fried rice/noodles.
