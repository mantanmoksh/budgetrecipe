Instructions

Step 1: Heat full-fat milk in a heavy-bottomed pan and bring it to a boil over medium heat. Stir constantly to prevent burning.

Step 2: Lower the heat and let the milk simmer, stirring occasionally, until it reduces to half its original volume.

Step 3: Add sugar and cardamom powder to the milk. Stir well until the sugar is completely dissolved.

Step 4: If using saffron, dissolve a pinch of strands in warm milk and add it to the mixture for added flavor and color.

Step 5: Allow the mixture to cool to room temperature. Pour it into kulfi molds or small airtight containers.

Step 6: Seal the molds with lids or cover with aluminum foil. Insert wooden sticks if desired.

Step 7: Freeze the kulfi molds for 6-8 hours, or until the kulfi is fully set.

Step 8: To serve, run the kulfi molds under warm water for a few seconds to easily release the frozen treat. Garnish with chopped nuts and enjoy!
