Instructions

Cook the noodles according to the package instructions until they are just cooked (al dente). Drain the water and rinse the noodles with cold water to stop the cooking process and prevent them from sticking. Set aside.
Heat oil in a wok or a large pan over high heat.
Add the thinly sliced onions and ginger-garlic paste to the hot oil. Stir-fry for a minute until the onions turn light golden brown.
Add the sliced capsicum and carrots to the wok. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender but still crisp.
Add the shredded cabbage to the wok and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes.
Push the vegetables to one side of the wok and add the cooked noodles to the empty space.
Pour soy sauce, vinegar, and red chili sauce (if using) over the noodles. Toss the noodles and vegetables together gently but thoroughly until everything is well combined and the noodles are evenly coated with the sauces.
Add salt to taste, keeping in mind that soy sauce already contains salt. Stir well.
Stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes, ensuring the noodles are heated through.
Garnish with chopped spring onions.
Serve hot immediately as a quick and tasty meal.
