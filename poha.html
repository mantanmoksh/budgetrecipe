Instructions

Prepare Poha: Rinse poha in water 2-3 times and drain completely. Sprinkle some water, cover and let it soften for 5 minutes. The poha should be soft but not mushy.
Tempering: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them crackle. Add peanuts and roast until golden. Add curry leaves, green chili and saut√© for few seconds.
Cook Onions: Add chopped onions and saut√© until translucent. Add turmeric powder and salt, mix well.
Combine: Add the softened poha and mix gently to combine all ingredients. Cook for 2-3 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally.
Serve: Turn off heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges. Serve hot with tea or as is.
