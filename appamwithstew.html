Instructions

Prepare Appam Batter: Soak raw rice for 4-6 hours. Grind soaked rice with grated coconut and a little water to a smooth batter. Add yeast and sugar, mix well, and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight until well-risen. Add salt to taste before cooking.
Cook Appam: Heat an appam pan or a small non-stick wok. Pour a ladleful of batter in the center, swirl to coat the sides, and cover. Cook on medium heat until the edges are lacy and the center is cooked through (about 2-3 minutes).
Prepare Vegetable Stew: Heat coconut oil in a pot. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and green chilies. Saut√© until light brown.
Add Vegetables: Add diced potatoes, carrots, and green beans. Saut√© for 5-7 minutes. Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
Simmer the Stew: Pour thin coconut milk and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the vegetables are cooked (about 15-20 minutes).
Add Thick Coconut Milk: Stir in thick coconut milk and green peas. Cook for another 2-3 minutes on low heat. Do not boil after adding thick coconut milk.
Garnish and Serve: Garnish the stew with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot appam with the vegetable stew. Enjoy!
