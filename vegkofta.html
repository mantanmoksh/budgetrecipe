Instructions (For Koftas)

In a bowl, combine the grated or mashed mixed vegetables, gram flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, chopped coriander leaves, and salt.
Mix well to form a soft dough. Adjust consistency with a little water or more gram flour/cornflour if needed.
Divide the dough into small balls (koftas).
Heat oil for frying in a pan over medium heat.
Gently fry the koftas until golden brown and crisp.
Remove and drain on paper towels.

Instructions (For Gravy)

Heat oil or ghee in another pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Sauté for a minute.
If using, grind soaked cashews to a paste and add to the pan. Cook for a minute.
Add water to get the desired gravy consistency. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
Stir in garam masala and salt. Add fresh cream if using.
Gently add the fried koftas to the gravy. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.
Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
