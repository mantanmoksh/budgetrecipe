Instructions

Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease a 9-inch loaf pan or bundt pan. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt. Set aside.
In a large bowl, whisk together honey and oil until well combined. Add eggs one at a time, whisking well after each addition. Stir in vanilla.
Alternately add the flour mixture and coffee/tea to the honey mixture, beginning and ending with the flour (flour-coffee-flour-coffee-flour). Mix until just combined - don't overmix.
Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake for 45-55 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean. If top browns too quickly, tent with foil.
Let cake cool in pan for 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack. For extra honey flavor, brush warm cake with 1-2 tablespoons additional honey. Cool completely before slicing. Store wrapped at room temperature for up to 3 days.
