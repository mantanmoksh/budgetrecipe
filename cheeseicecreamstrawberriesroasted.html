Instructions

Roast the strawberries: Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Toss strawberries with brown sugar and balsamic vinegar. Spread on baking sheet and roast for 25-30 minutes until caramelized. Let cool completely.
Make ice cream base: Beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually beat in condensed milk until fully incorporated. Mix in vanilla.
Whip the cream: In separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks. Gently fold into cream cheese mixture.
Prepare strawberries: Mash half the roasted strawberries with their juices. Keep remaining berries whole or roughly chop.
Layer in container: In loaf pan or freezer container, alternate layers of ice cream base, mashed strawberries, and graham crumbs. Swirl gently with knife.
Add whole berries: Fold in reserved roasted strawberry pieces throughout the mixture.
Freeze: Cover with parchment paper pressed directly on surface. Freeze at least 6 hours, preferably overnight.
Serve: Let sit at room temperature 5-10 minutes before scooping. Garnish with extra graham crumbs and fresh strawberries if desired.
