Instructions

Prepare Ginger: Wash and peel the ginger root. You can slice it thinly, crush it with the back of a knife, or grate it for stronger flavor. More surface area means more flavor extraction.
Boil Water: In a small saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add the prepared ginger (and turmeric if using). Reduce heat and simmer for 5-10 minutes depending on how strong you want the ginger flavor.
Strain: Turn off the heat and strain the tea into your cup, removing the ginger pieces. For stronger tea, let it steep for 2-3 minutes before straining.
Add Flavors: Stir in lemon juice and honey while the tea is still hot. Adjust quantities to taste - more lemon for tartness, more honey for sweetness.
Serve: Garnish with a lemon slice. Enjoy hot for maximum benefits. For iced version, let cool then refrigerate and serve over ice.
