Instructions

Take the cubed boiled potatoes in a bowl. Add salt to taste and gently toss.
Arrange the seasoned potato cubes on a plate or in a bowl.
Drizzle whisked plain yogurt generously over the potatoes.
Spoon the tamarind chutney and green chutney over the yogurt. Adjust the amount according to your taste preference.
Sprinkle finely chopped onions and boiled chickpeas over the chutneys.
Add red chili powder, roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, and black salt.
Top with crispy fried sev and finely chopped coriander leaves.
Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious aloo chaat! You can add other toppings like chopped tomatoes or a sprinkle of lemon juice if desired.
